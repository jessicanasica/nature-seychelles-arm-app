Cousin Island ARM - Privacy Policy

App for Reserve Management by Nature Seychelles

Last updated: April 20, 2026

1. Introduction

Cousin Island ARM ("the App") is a conservation field work application developed by Nature Seychelles for the management and monitoring of Cousin Island Special Reserve. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the App.

2. Information We Collect

The App collects the following types of information to support reserve management activities:

  • Account Information: Your name and email address, used for authentication and identifying data submissions.
  • Location Data: GPS coordinates captured when recording field observations, turtle nesting surveys, seabird monitoring (SMR), beach profiles, and other monitoring activities. Location is only collected when you actively use the App's monitoring features.
  • Photos: Images captured through the App's camera for documenting wildlife observations, nesting activities, habitat conditions, and environmental monitoring.
  • Conservation Monitoring Data: Field observations including turtle nesting data, seabird monitoring records (Seabird Monitoring Record), beach cleaning logs, vegetation surveys, GPS tracking data, calendar events, and other ecological data you enter into the App.
  • Device Information: Basic device identifiers for managing offline data synchronisation and over-the-air updates.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively for:

  • Recording and storing conservation monitoring data for Cousin Island Special Reserve management.
  • Generating reports and statistics for Nature Seychelles conservation programmes.
  • Synchronising your offline data with our servers when an internet connection is available.
  • Identifying data submissions by field team members.
  • Delivering over-the-air application updates.

4. Data Storage and Security

Monitoring data is stored locally on your device when offline and synchronised to our secure servers (hosted on Supabase) when connectivity is available. All data transmissions are encrypted using HTTPS. Access to the data is restricted to authorised members of the Nature Seychelles reserve management team.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Conservation monitoring data may be used in aggregated, anonymised form for scientific publications, conservation reports, and programme evaluations by Nature Seychelles and its partners.

6. Camera and Photo Library Access

The App requests access to your device's camera and photo library solely for capturing and attaching photos to monitoring reports. Photos are stored locally and uploaded to our servers as part of your field reports. We do not access your personal photos outside of the App.

7. Location Services

The App uses location services to record GPS coordinates for monitoring sites, turtle nesting surveys, and field observations. Location data is captured when you actively use monitoring features. Background location may be used during GPS tracking sessions for specific field activities, and only while the App is in active use for those tasks.

8. Bluetooth

The App may use Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) to communicate with external GPS devices and environmental sensors used during field work. No personal data is collected or transmitted via Bluetooth.

9. Offline Data

The App is designed to work offline on Cousin Island where internet connectivity is limited. Data entered while offline is stored securely on your device and queued for upload when an internet connection becomes available.

10. Data Retention

Monitoring data is retained for the duration of the Nature Seychelles conservation programme on Cousin Island. You may request deletion of your personal account information by contacting us at the email address below.

11. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for use by authorised reserve management staff and conservation volunteers, and is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
